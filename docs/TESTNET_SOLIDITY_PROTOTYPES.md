# Testnet Solidity Prototypes

The Solidity contracts under `quantos/solidity-contracts/` are testnet prototypes.

They exist to validate product flows such as:

- Staking and liquid staking.
- Restaking.
- Insurance pool mechanics.
- DAO and token workflows.

These contracts should not be treated as final mainnet smart contracts. They may be reviewed for context, but production mainnet contract review should focus on the future Rust resource-based implementation.
